Our BSEH Alumni Success

Morgan Brown '96

Career: Environmental Health and Safety Specialist at Mayne Pharma, Inc., Greenville, NC

What are your responsibilities in your current EH job?
My responsibilities include managing environmental permitting programs (waste water, storm water, air permit, hazardous waste); ensuring laboratory safety through inspections; providing trainings to employees as needed including forklift training, emergency equipment, hazard communication; identifying and correcting fire and life safety hazards through inspections and employee reports; complying with OSHA regulations; and creating a welcoming culture of occupational safety and health.

What do you like most about your career?
I like most that my career is never the same. Some days I will sit at my desk all day working on reports, but without warning my day can change into rectifying a hazard that I noticed while walking back from lunch. There is always something to do and always something new to learn.

How do you think your ECU EH education prepared you for your career and/or graduate school?
"My EHS degree has DRASTICALLY helped me in my career. With the exception of one or two classes, I am using information from the entire program almost daily. After working for a year, I decided to begin my graduate degree studies at ECU through the MS Occupational Safety program. My undergraduate classes continue to benefit me in the program since I had a good foundation of knowledge. I am set to graduate in December [2016]."

"My freshman year I took the Intro to EHS course with Mr. William Hill because I needed an elective. That random class has changed my entire life and helped me find a career and future that I am not only good at, but I am passionate and proud to be practicing in this field. I am teaching and helping employees to work safely so they can have a better tomorrow."
